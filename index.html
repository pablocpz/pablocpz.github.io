Featured Work

Neural Network ilustrated & coded from scratch: Here I made a handwritten + code guide of making a neural network from scratch. It was a result of an intense & funny summer I spent in 2023, learning all the core math of deep learning, helping me to solve high school errors and take a step further. I learnt:

  • Calculus (derivatives, geometric interpretation, integrals and limits)
  • Linear Algebra (arithmetic operations between matrices, vectors, scalars) and basic concepts like determinants
  • Furthemore, solved some school concepts I used to confuse (like trigonometrics), by applying intuitive and interactive visualizations in python notebooks

Game of life coded with pygame: Here I documented a step-by-step guide of coding the popular John Conway's Game of Life using the pygame library. This taught me about cellular automaton and the concept of Turing completeness, which indicates that with certain rules, any algorithm's logic can be simulated.

Fractals with L-systems: Here I studied the process of coding some popular fractals using L-systems. It is a parallel rewriting system, often used to model the growth processes of plant development.
